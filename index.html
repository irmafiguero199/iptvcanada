IPTV technology is completely legal in Canada. Major Canadian telecom companies like Bell and Rogers use IPTV technology to deliver their own services. How do I set up IPTV on Firestick in Canada? +
Setup takes under 5 minutes: 1) Go to Firestick Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options → Enable "Apps from Unknown Sources". 2) Download the Downloader app from the Amazon Store. 3) Enter our app download URL (sent in your welcome email). 4) Install the NorthStream app. 5) Enter your username and password. 6) Done — start watching!
